Privacy & Data

What we collect

We store your name, email, and event/guest data that you create. We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or share your information with third parties.

Who can see your data

Your guest list and events are private to you. When you share an event with a co-host, they can see the guest list for that event only. Viewers can see but not edit.

Data deletion

You can delete any event, guest, or tag at any time. Deleted items are kept in "Recently Deleted" for 30 days, then permanently removed. To delete your entire account, contact us.

Security

Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. All connections use HTTPS encryption. We use CSRF protection on all forms.
